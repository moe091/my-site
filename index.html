About Me

Read my Blog to learn more about what I do

My passion for creating things is what first drew me into programming. By age 10 I was already a computer nerd, but I had yet to discover the possibilities of programming until I came across a VB6 "Hello World" tutorial on a game modding forum. I immediately got to work installing VB and completing the tutorial and was amazed when my program actually worked. I was instantly hooked, before long I had way more ideas for software projects than I could ever realistically complete. That's when my passion for creating things and desire to learn more took over.

I made a lot of games early on, but it wasn't long before I started coming up with all sorts of ideas for different types of software: websites, tools, and eventually mobile apps. I went through a phase for pretty much every type of software development at one point, but eventually settled on Web Development(with game dev as a secondary side/hobby interest). I want to create software that is accessible, used by a lot of people, on a platform that doesn't limit the possibilities of what I can create; overall the web is the perfect choice for me. At this point I think I can say I'm a pretty capable and talented developer, I'm very well-rounded and have all the basics mastered as well as some of the more advanced topics. Luckily, my desire to create better, more complex software will always drive me to keep learning, meaning my skills won't stop improving any time soon.
